Crwn Clothing

Crwn-Clothing was an attempt at making something that can actually be used in the real world. Built in React, crwn-clothing made me think like a developer working on a team where I had to consider how my project was organized and that other people would be making contributions, since my first web application I have made a point to treat everything I build as if I was working on it with other people. the app itself, displays a wide range of products in a way that is clean, modern and easy to navigate. Clipz

Clipz is a passion project of mine, born out of my love for gaming and my interest in full stack web development. I decided to take on the challenge of learning another new framework.. Angular. However I wanted to make something a little more fun as well as personal. This project ended up becoming very difficult as I was attempting to use many external libraries in order to make it function rather than building everything from scratch. This taught me a very valuable lesson when it came to troubleshooting. As difficult as it became, I couldn't be more proud of the work I put into it. Unfortunately though I did not deploy the application because of the costs of maintaining a database capable of holding a potentially high number of video files. Feel free to check out the source code anyway!
